Terragotchi tldr

Tend to your own pocket-sized Earth by learning and practicing sustainable behaviors and mindsets, supported by constructive strategies for navigating climate anxiety.

Overview

Terragotchi is a concept app researched, designed, and developed between January-May 2025 as my Capstone Project for the UX Design Masters Program at the Maryland Institute College of Art. The app combines resource wiki, habit tracking, mindfulness, and virtual-pet gamification to educate, encourage, and strengthen sustainable lifestyles while providing guidance on navigating climate anxiety.
